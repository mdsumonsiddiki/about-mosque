Our Beliefs

We believe that God is Divine, Just, Ever-Forgiving, Merciful and Most Gracious. Our beliefs reflect the sacred thoughts of major historic religious doctrines. We respect and love all of God’s prophets and messengers, starting from Adam and ending with the Prophet Mohammed (Peace ).

We are open-minded in accepting people with different religious beliefs as brothers and sisters, based on our common humanity and the conviction that the closest to God is the most God conscious.

At the Islamic Center of Cape Girardeau, we believe in assisting members and families in building a rewarding life and hereafter. We aim to help parents guide their children to the right path in life. We also support couples and newly-weds to nourish their relationships by strengthening their trust in Allah.

ICofCG provides members the knowledge and the opportunities to serve each other as well as their community. We believe that we all are the people of God that follow the guidance of His prophets and messengers. We understand there are many areas in our community that need to improve, and we are glad to be here to support.
